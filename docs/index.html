THE FIVE LARGEST PROTESTS IN U.S. HISTORY

The tradition of protests that demand positive change in the United States continues.

Here are five of the top protests in terms of attendance.

  1. George Floyd protests nationwide in 2020 with an estimated 26 million protestors.
  2. Earth Day protests in 1970 with 20 million nationwide.
  3. Women's March in 2017 drew about 5.6 million people nationwide.
  4. March for our lives, a student-led protest in 2018 against gun violence, drew around 2 million people.
  5. Women's March brought an estimated 1.5 million people in 2018.
List complied from Wikipedia.
